Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 11, 2026

NutriSkan is designed to be privacy-first. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Website: We do not use analytics trackers on this landing page.
  • App: The app may request camera permission to scan barcodes. We do not require an account.

How barcode data is used

When you scan a barcode, the app may use that barcode to look up product information from public data sources to provide a score and explanations.

Data storage

Scan history (if enabled) is stored on your device. We do not sell personal information.

Contact

Email support@nutriskan.com with privacy questions.
